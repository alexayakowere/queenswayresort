Kisumu Dala:

Nestled along the serene shores of Lake Victoria in western Kenya, Kisumu emerges as a captivating destination, rich in both natural beauty and historical significance. This vibrant city has evolved into a cultural melting pot, where the echoes of its past reverberate alongside the tranquil waters. Kisumu boasts a history intertwined with trade, fishing, and the vibrant Luo community, creating a tapestry of traditions.

As the waters of Lake Victoria lap against its shores, Kisumu invites exploration with a magnetic charm. Immerse yourself in this unique blend of history and tranquility at Queens Way Resort, our haven nestled in the heart of Kisumu. Offering luxury amid the city's rich narratives, our resort beckons you to unwind in comfort while experiencing the cultural richness that defines Kisumu's heritage. Let Queens Way Resort be your gateway to a harmonious retreat where tradition and modernity dance in perfect balance.


Nestled in the heart of Kisumu, Queens Way Resort offers an idyllic retreat. Accessible through public entrances, our charming resort, accommodates and offers various activities that provide a tranquil escape within proximity.

Effortless exploration in town is ensured with convenient transportation to and from Queens Way Resort, while ample parking is available for those who prefer to drive. Lovely gardens set the stage for small events like birthdays, photoshoots, or intimate group retreats.
